# Vektorový součin

## Summary
For a list of vectors $(x_1,..., x_{n-1})$, the vector product satisfies $(\times(x_1,..., x_{n-1}) | x) = \text{det}(x_1,...,x_{n-1},x)$ for all $x$ in $\text{R}^n$.
